simplifiCOACHing® is a unique LEARNing approach that effectively changes the Social Condition within organizations to be more inclusive and harmonious, resulting in better work habits, voluntary discipline and sustainable improvements for all. It is the heartbeat of the COACH Approach®, a proven system of management that transforms organizations by transforming the people within them.

simplifiCOACHing® is recognized by global, regional and local companies as well as respected Universities.

In 2016 and 2017 simplifiCOACHing® will be offered...

  • -to more Learning Institutions as a Post Grad Elective Course.
  • -to Training Organizations that want to elevate their effectiveness from 'transfer of knowledge' to 'sustainable improvement through practical application'.
  • -to Individuals World-Wide as a web-based self-improvement approach.
