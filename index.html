About Coach Business Simplification

COACH specializes in business simplification. Simplification of management processes, simplification of communication between departments and between individuals, simplification of planning and performance review forums.

What is the Coach Approach®

The COACH Approach® is a careful balance of Technical and Tactical Approaches.
Every client receives all of the nine statements below. The first three statements are technical,
the last three are tactical, and the middle three are both.



  • Simplification of management processes at all levels,
  • Simplification of communication between departments and between individuals,
  • Simplification of planning and performace review forums,
  • Systematic improvement in attitudes, opinions, knowledge, skill and judgment,
  • Systematic improvement in work habits,
  • Systematic improvement in the alignment of resources to output volumes
  • Building individual confidence, competence, work ethic and positive attitude
  • Building group confidence, competence, teamwork and competitiveness
  • Building organizational confidence, competence, and ambition
